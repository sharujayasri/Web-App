About Us

CardioLume is an AI-powered platform designed to predict cardiovascular disease risk and promote heart health. Using advanced machine learning algorithms, our system analyzes key health parameters to provide personalized risk assessments and actionable lifestyle recommendations.

Our goal is to enable early detection, encourage preventive care, and support better heart health decisions. With a secure and user-friendly approach, we help individuals take control of their well-being through AI-driven insights and expert-backed guidance.
