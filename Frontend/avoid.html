❌ Foods to Avoid

Processed Foods – High in preservatives, trans fats, and additives.
Sugary Foods – Sodas, pastries, and candies spike blood sugar levels.
Fried Foods – French fries, chips, and fried chicken increase cholesterol.
Red Meat – High in saturated fats, leading to heart disease.
High-Sodium Foods – Excess salt raises blood pressure, affecting heart health.
