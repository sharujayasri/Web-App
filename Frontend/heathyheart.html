✅ Heart-Healthy Foods

Fruits & Vegetables – Rich in vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants.
Whole Grains – Brown rice, quinoa, oats, whole wheat bread.
Healthy Fats – Olive oil, nuts, seeds, avocados.
Lean Proteins – Fish (especially fatty fish like salmon), skinless poultry, beans, and legumes.
Low-Fat Dairy – Yogurt, milk, and cheese (low in saturated fat).
